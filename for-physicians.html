For Physicians

An independent registered nurse advocate for your most complex patients.

32 years of bedside experience. Independent of hospital, insurance, and system affiliations. Built to extend your reach when families need more support than your schedule allows.

Translate

I sit with families after rounds and re-explain everything in language they can hold. I document every conversation so the family is not relying on memory at 2am.

Stabilize

I provide nervous-system regulation to families running on adrenaline and no sleep — so they can hear, integrate, and consent clearly when you need them to.

Coordinate

I track specialist consults, follow-up appointments, and post-discharge logistics. I fill the gaps that case management cannot always cover.

For Your Team

What I do alongside you.

  • Reduce family confusion and repeated questions
  • Accelerate informed consent for complex procedures
  • Improve discharge readiness and reduce readmission risk
  • Provide one consistent family point of contact across shifts
  • Document outside the chart, on the family's behalf
  • Refuse to interfere with clinical decisions, ever

How to Refer

Three ways forward.

1

Submit the referral form.

The fastest, most documented path. Complete the brief intake form below and I will reach out to you and the family within the hour during business hours, within three hours after-hours.

2

Mention me to the family by name.

Suggest the family consider an independent patient advocate, and provide my card or the URL theclinicaldoula.com. I take it from there.

3

Email or text me directly.

If urgency requires it, you can reach out yourself with the family's permission. I will respond within the hour and coordinate the family contact directly. My role is alongside yours, never instead of it.
